Brainhack Global

The Brainhack DC 2020 is an official satellite event of the International Brainhack Global. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with diverse backgrounds to collaborate on science projects in neuroimaging.

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to the official Brainhack Project Gallery and to submit a report describing their project as a conference paper to the 2020 Brainhack Proceedings after the event.

All participants should read and adhere to the Brainhack Code of Conduct.
